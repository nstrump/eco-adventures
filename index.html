What We Do

Scuba Diving

Advanced exploration for experienced divers.

  • Skill Level: Advanced
  • Group Size: 1–5
  • Day and night tours
  • 2-3 Assistant Divers

Snorkeling

Snorkeling in globally unique areas.

  • Skill Level: Novice
  • Group Size: 1–15
  • Day and night tours
  • 2-5 Assistant Snorkelers

Kayaking

Novice and experienced alike will thrill at our waters.

  • Skill Level: Novice
  • Group Size: 1–10
  • Day and night tours
  • 1-4 Assistant Kayakers

Camping

Site camp while preserving the local environment.

  • Skill Level: Novice
  • Group Size: 1–20
  • 3-14 day outings
  • 2-3 Assistant Campers

Hiking

Hikers of all abilities, we've found your path.

  • Skill Level: Intermediate
  • Group Size: 1–20
  • Day & multi-week trips
  • 1-5 Assistant Guides

Mountain Biking

Hit some bucket list trails.

  • Skill Level: Advanced
  • Group Size: 1–5
  • Day tours only
  • 2-3 Assistant Riders
